Unscramble Words

Instructions:

The rules of the game - Different wrong spelt words will be given, and a clue to extract the correct word from the given word will be provided respectively. Input your answer in the box below the clue and click next to move to next question.
You have a minute to answer each question, after which it skips to the next question.

Example:

HOPNE

Clue: Used for making calls.
